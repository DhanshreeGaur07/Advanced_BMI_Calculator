The BMI Formula

Metric:

BMI = weight(kg) ÷ height(m)²

Imperial:

BMI = 703 × weight(lb) ÷ height(in)²

BMI is a screening tool, not a diagnostic measure. It does not account for age, sex, ethnicity, or muscle mass. Always consult a healthcare professional for a complete health assessment.

BMI is for screening purposes only. Not a substitute for medical advice.
